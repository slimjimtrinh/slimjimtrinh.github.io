Finding a job

Your Journey Starts With You

Finding a job may seem very hectic but with 6 few simple steps, finding a job may just seem easy. Jobs are getting more and more competative to get without the right expectations and requirements for the company. If you are able to make yourself appear more professional beacuse you know the back and in's of the employer industry, you can land a job in no time. Jobjourneys goal is to educate new job seeker or those who are stuggling to find a job, and making sure you do not make those simple mistakes when job searching.

Here are 6 simple steps you should go through to make sure you land your desired job.

  1. Find your interest
  2. Use those interest to search for a job
  3. Create a resume and cover letter that fits that job opening
  4. Apply with honesty
  5. Prepare and succeed with your interview
  6. Tips and be patient
