Most commonly asked questions

Question: Should I apply to a job where I might not have all the qualifications?

Answer: Yes! Even if you don't have all the requirements, you can still have a chance get the job and learn on site. If you are willing to put in the work to learn the new skill, go for it.

Question: How long should my cover letter be?

Answer: Your cover letter should be limited to one page, you should be able to persuade the reader enough with you skills and history.

Question: I'm applying for a job and it's asking for my SSN, do I trust it?

Answer: Do some research on the company and see if the listing is legit. If you are filling it out at a public place, make sure your connection is safe. Check your URL for the https:// and secure icon at the begining of the sites URL to make sure that website is secured. If its not, I recommend that you go to the job company and as for an appicaiton there.

Question: I had an interview and haven't got a call back yet, what do I do???

Answer: If you haven't got called back in 2-3 days, sometimes its just the company not having time to review yet but always follow up with them when you can.

Question: I'm looking for an intern, what's the process?

Answer: The process for finding an internship is well the same as finding a job. Many job searching sites include internships as an option.
