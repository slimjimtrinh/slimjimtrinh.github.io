Top ten tips
  1. Don't stress, the biggest problem people have is being over stressed and worried, try to relax as you make less mistakes that way.
  2. Revise your work - The most common mistake is making those small typos that employers will see, always double check your work. Even if you have to ask another friend or family member to do so.
  3. Research interview questions in advanced and practice answering them.
  4. Be on time, it is much better to arrive early than showing up late.
  5. Turn your phone off or make it silent, if your phone goes off during the interview, you chances of getting that job are slim to none.
  6. Aways ask questions, be prepared to have some question ready so that you aren't there thoughtless.
